Veteran-Owned · Family-Run

Honest RV Repair, Built on Service

Triumphant RV Solutions was founded on a simple frustration: RV owners shouldn't have to surrender their rig for weeks — or wonder if they're being told the truth about what's wrong with it.

From the Navy to Your Campsite

James Barber served in the U.S. Navy, and he runs this company the way the service taught him: do the job right, tell the truth, take care of people.

After becoming an RVTAA-certified technician, James and his wife Brenda built Triumphant RV Solutions to give North Georgia RVers something rare — expert repair that comes to you, with pricing you see before the work starts.

Credentials That Matter

Our technicians hold RVTAA certification and NRVTA training — the industry's recognized standards for RV service. And we're fully insured, so you're protected every step of the way.

The Barber Family

When they're not keeping North Georgia's RVs rolling, James and Brenda travel and sing with their daughter Rebecca as The Barber Family — a gospel music ministry that's taken them across the Southeast. Faith, family, and honest work: that's the foundation everything here is built on.
