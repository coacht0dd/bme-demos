We Put the Fun Back in RVing

We're James & Brenda Barber — and we built Triumphant RV Solutions to be your one-stop shop for making your day TRIUMPHANT.

Our Story

We know how expensive and time-consuming it is to have your RV repaired — especially when you have to take it somewhere and leave it. That never sat right with us. With Triumphant, you get to keep your RV while we repair it. We come to your campsite, your storage lot, or your driveway, and we treat your rig like it's our own.

James is a U.S. Navy veteran, evangelist, author, singer/songwriter, and RVTAA-Certified Technician. He runs this company the way the Navy taught him: do the job right, tell the truth, and take care of people. Brenda keeps everything — and everyone — running. Together, we provide transparent pricing and the kind of customer service that's getting hard to find.

We strive to provide a TRIUMPHANT solution to EVERY RV problem. That's not a slogan — it's how we answer the phone.
