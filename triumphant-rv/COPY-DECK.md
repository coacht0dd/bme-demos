**H1:** RV Water Heater Repair — Hot Showers, Restored
**Answer block:** We repair and service RV water heaters at your location anywhere within about an hour of Gainesville, GA — pilot and igniter problems, anode rods, element replacement, and full two-step flush service on all major brands.

**Call us when…** no hot water on gas, electric, or both · pilot won't stay lit · water smells like rotten eggs · popping or rumbling from the tank · leaks at the pressure relief valve · lukewarm water that runs out fast

**What we do:** Complete water heater service: igniter and thermostat repair, heating element replacement, anode rod inspection and replacement, and our two-step tank flush that clears the sediment robbing your tank of capacity and lifespan. **Why mobile matters:** Cold showers get old fast — especially for full-timers. We restore hot water at your site, usually in a single visit.

**FAQ:**
- **Why does my RV hot water smell like rotten eggs?** That's usually a failing anode rod reacting with bacteria in the tank. We replace the rod and sanitize the system on-site — the smell goes with it.
- **How often should the anode rod be replaced?** Check it annually; replace when it's more than half consumed. It's a cheap part that protects an expensive tank — we check it in every maintenance package.
- **Do you work on tankless RV water heaters?** Yes — we service tankless units like Girard and Furrion as well as traditional Suburban and Atwood/Dometic tank heaters.
