Campground Questions

Can you repair my RV inside a campground or state park?
Yes. Our technicians service RVs on-site at campgrounds, Corps parks, and state parks across North Georgia. Just give us the campground name and your site number when you call.
Which Lake Lanier campgrounds do you cover?
All of them — Duckett Mill, Bolding Mill, Old Federal, Van Pugh, Shoal Creek, Shady Grove, Bald Ridge Creek, War Hill, River Forks, Don Carter State Park, and Margaritaville at Lanier Islands.
My campground isn't listed — can you still come?
Probably. The list covers the campgrounds we visit most, not the limit of where we go. If you're within about an hour of Gainesville, call (770) 519-2828 and we'll confirm.
