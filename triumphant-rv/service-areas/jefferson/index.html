Things to Do in Jefferson

Jefferson's claim to fame is medical history — Dr. Crawford W. Long performed the first surgery using ether anesthesia here on March 30, 1842 — celebrated alongside a historic square and the shoals of Hurricane Shoals Park.

Hurricane Shoals Park

Cascading shoals with a covered bridge, grist mill, tubing, and a heritage village of relocated historic buildings.

Visit site ↗

Crawford W. Long Museum

The discovery of surgical anesthesia, period medical artifacts, and an old-fashioned general store.

Visit site ↗

Historic Downtown Jefferson

A preserved square of boutiques and antique stores.

Visit site ↗

Shields-Ethridge Heritage Farm

A preserved agricultural heritage site and outdoor museum near town.
