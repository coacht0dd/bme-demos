Mobile RV Repair at Lake Lanier

Triumphant RV Solutions provides mobile RV repair and maintenance at Lake Lanier campgrounds, marinas, and residences — usually within a short drive from our Gainesville base. Certified, veteran-owned, and available 24/7 for emergencies.

Lake Lanier is one of the busiest RV destinations in Georgia, and we're right next door. From Margaritaville at Lanier Islands to the Corps of Engineers campgrounds — Bald Ridge Creek, Duckett Mill, Bolding Mill, Old Federal, War Hill — if your rig has a problem lakeside, our technicians have fixed that exact problem in that exact campground loop. Being based in Gainesville means we're often on-site faster than you could get a tow truck scheduled.

Drive time: Most Lake Lanier campgrounds are 15–30 minutes from our base — trip fee typically included.

Things to Do in Lake Lanier

A 38,000-acre Corps of Engineers reservoir with 690+ miles of shoreline, Lanier draws roughly 11.8 million visitors a year — the most-visited Corps lake in America — set against the blue foothills of the Blue Ridge.

Margaritaville at Lanier Islands

Fins Up Water Park, LandShark Landing, beaches, boat rentals, and winter's License to Chill Snow Island.
