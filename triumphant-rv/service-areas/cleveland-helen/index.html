Serving Cleveland & Helen

Mobile RV Repair in Cleveland & Helen

Triumphant RV Solutions serves Cleveland, Helen, and White County with mobile RV repair — including Unicoi State Park and the Helen-area campgrounds — about 40 minutes from our Gainesville base.

Helen's Bavarian charm draws RVers from across the Southeast — and Oktoberfest season packs every campground from Unicoi State Park to the private parks along the Chattahoochee, plus Leisure Acres down in Cleveland. When your rig acts up mid-vacation in the mountains, the last thing you want is a tow down GA-75. Our technicians come up regularly for A/C failures in summer, furnace calls in leaf season, and everything between.

Drive time: About 40 minutes from Gainesville.

Things to Do in Cleveland & Helen

Helen is a re-created Bavarian alpine village on the Chattahoochee — home to the longest-running Oktoberfest in the United States, held since 1970 — and nearby Cleveland is the birthplace of the Cabbage Patch Kids.

Unicoi State Park & Lodge

1,050 acres with a lake beach, ziplining, fly fishing, and an RV-friendly campground two miles from Helen.

Anna Ruby Falls

Tubing the Chattahoochee

Helen's Oktoberfest
