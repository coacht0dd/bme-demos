Things to Do in Braselton

Spanning four counties, Braselton pairs a preserved early-1900s downtown with two heavyweight draws: a French-château winery resort and Michelin Raceway Road Atlanta — 320,000+ visitors a year, 140,000+ at Petit Le Mans alone.

Michelin Raceway Road Atlanta

A 2.54-mile, 12-turn road course on 750 acres — home of the Motul Petit Le Mans endurance classic.
