Things to Do in Gainesville

The “Queen City of the Mountains” sits at Lake Lanier's north end — and every November, downtown kicks off the holidays by lighting a 25-foot metal chicken at the Jingle Mingle festival. Poultry Capital of the World, and proud of it.

Don Carter State Park

The only state park on Lake Lanier — sandy swim beach, 12+ miles of trails, and a 44-site RV campground with water/electric hookups.

Lake Lanier Olympic Park

The 1996 Olympic rowing venue, now open for kayak rentals, a small beach, and lakeside events.

Northeast Georgia History Center

10,000+ years of regional history with the White Path Cabin and a working blacksmith shop.

Downtown Gainesville Square

Boutiques, breweries, restaurants, and a Friday farmers market from May on.
