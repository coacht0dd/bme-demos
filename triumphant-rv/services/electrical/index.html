RV Electrical Repair — We Trace It, We Fix It

Triumphant RV Solutions diagnoses and repairs RV electrical systems on-site across North Georgia — shore power, converters, inverters, batteries, and 12V/120V circuits — with certified technicians who find the real fault instead of guessing.

Call Us When…

  • Outlets are dead but the breaker looks fine
  • Batteries drain overnight
  • The converter hums or clicks
  • Lights flicker or dim
  • Shore power won't connect
  • Fuses keep blowing

What We Do

Systematic electrical diagnosis across both your 12-volt and 120-volt systems: converters, inverters, transfer switches, battery banks and charging, shore power connections, breakers, and wiring faults. Electrical gremlins are the most misdiagnosed problems in RVs — we trace them to the source.

Why Mobile Matters

Electrical faults are intermittent — they hide on the drive to a shop and reappear at the campsite. Diagnosing where the problem actually happens is half the battle.

Electrical Systems Questions

Why do my RV batteries die overnight?
Usually a parasitic draw, a failing converter, or batteries past their life. We test the whole charging chain at your site and show you exactly what we found.
Can you fix shore power problems at my campsite?
Yes — pedestal connection issues, transfer switches, and power protection faults are common calls. We diagnose on-site and quote before repairing.
Do you install upgrades like inverters or surge protection?
Yes — ask when you call. We install and configure inverters, hardwired surge protection, and battery upgrades.
