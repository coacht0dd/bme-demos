Water Heaters Questions

Why does my RV hot water smell like rotten eggs?
That's usually a failing anode rod reacting with bacteria in the tank. We replace the rod and sanitize the system on-site — the smell goes with it.
How often should the anode rod be replaced?
Check it annually; replace when it's more than half consumed. It's a cheap part that protects an expensive tank — we check it in every maintenance package.
Do you work on tankless RV water heaters?
Yes — we service tankless units like Girard and Furrion as well as traditional Suburban and Atwood/Dometic tank heaters.
