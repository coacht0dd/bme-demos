RV Air Conditioning & Heating Repair — At Your Campsite

Triumphant RV Solutions repairs RV air conditioners and furnaces on-site anywhere within about an hour of Gainesville, GA. Certified diagnosis, honest pricing, and most fixes done the same visit — no towing, no waiting weeks at a dealer.

Call Us When…

  • Your A/C runs but won't cool
  • It trips the breaker
  • Airflow is weak
  • The furnace won't ignite
  • The system short-cycles on and off
  • Rattling or grinding from the rooftop unit

What We Do

Full diagnosis and repair of rooftop A/C units, heat pumps, and furnaces — capacitors, compressors, control boards, thermostats, ductwork, igniters, and sail switches. We service all major brands, and older units don't scare us: one recent customer's 2000 Prevost left our visit with its A/C running like new.

Why Mobile Matters

A dead A/C in a Georgia summer isn't an inconvenience — it can end your trip or make your home unlivable. Our technicians come to you, usually with the parts to fix it on the spot.

A/C & Heating Questions

Can you fix my RV A/C at the campground?
Yes — that's what we do. Our technicians repair RV air conditioners on-site at campgrounds and RV parks across North Georgia, including Lake Lanier. Most repairs are completed in one visit.
Should I repair or replace my RV A/C?
If the unit is under 8–10 years old, repair usually wins. We diagnose first and give you an honest recommendation with real numbers — we don't sell you a new unit you don't need.
Do you service RV furnaces too?
Yes. We diagnose and repair RV furnaces and heating systems — igniters, sail switches, control boards — so you're warm on the road year-round.
