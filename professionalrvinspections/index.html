Straight Answers

Questions RV Buyers Ask Us

Do I need to be present for the inspection in Illinois or Wisconsin?

No — you don’t need to be there. PRO RVI inspects at the seller’s location and delivers a full verbal walkthrough plus a detailed written report within 24 hours. Many clients are buying remotely and have never seen the RV in person before inspection.

How long does a full RV inspection take near Chicago?

A Safety Inspection takes 2–3 hours. A full Pre-Purchase Inspection on a loaded Class A diesel motorhome with fluid samples can take 8–10 hours. We don’t rush — every system is actually tested, not just visually scanned.

Will RV dealers in Illinois or Indiana allow an independent inspection?

Yes — most reputable dealers have been very accommodating. PRO RVI has inspected at 100+ dealerships across Illinois, Wisconsin, Indiana, Michigan, and Iowa. If a dealer resists an independent inspection, that itself is worth knowing.

Can you inspect an RV in Michigan or Ohio if I’m buying remotely?

Absolutely — that’s a major part of our business. Clients from all 50 states and countries including Australia, Canada, England, and Sweden have hired PRO RVI to inspect before traveling to see the RV.

Is your inspection the same as the dealer’s pre-delivery inspection (PDI)?

No — and that’s the point. A dealer’s PDI is done by someone with a financial interest in completing the sale. PRO RVI is fully independent. We don’t do repairs, so we have no incentive to find — or overlook — anything.
