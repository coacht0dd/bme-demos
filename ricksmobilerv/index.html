The People Behind The Wrench

Fixing RVs Is What We Do. Honesty Is Who We Are.

Rick's Mobile RV Services is owned and operated by Rick & Diann Root of Palm Beach County. After buying their first RV in 2018, they learned firsthand how hard it is to find a certified, licensed, and insured technician you can trust — so Rick built a whole new career fixing that.

A U.S. Air Force veteran and retired law enforcement lieutenant, Rick trained at the National RV Training Academy and earned his RVTAA technician and NRVIA inspector certifications. Honor, integrity, and accountability aren't slogans here — they're how every job gets done.

Straight Answers

Questions RVers Ask Us

Do you really come to my campground?

Yes. Rick's Mobile RV Services is 100% mobile — we repair, maintain, and inspect your RV at your home, campground, or storage unit anywhere in Palm Beach, Broward, and Martin County. You never haul it to a shop.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Boca Raton and cover Palm Beach County, Broward County, and Martin County — roughly a 50-mile radius. Just outside it? Call (561) 237-5029; we can often still help for a small per-mile fee.

Can you fix bad tank odors or a clog?

Yes. Our hydro-jetting equipment safely clears struvite, sludge, misreading sensors, clogs, and even poop pyramids from your black and gray tanks and piping — a specialty most techs won't touch.

Do you give prices up front?

Yes. Rick's pricing is straightforward and posted — service calls, mobile fees, and tank cleaning are all transparent, with no hidden costs and no extra charge for weekend appointments.

Are you certified and insured?

Yes. Rick Root is an RVTAA-certified technician and NRVIA-certified inspector, trained at the National RV Training Academy, and the business is fully licensed and insured across South Florida.
