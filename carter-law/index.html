Straight Answers

Questions Injured People Ask Us

Do I even have a case?

If someone else's carelessness caused your injury, you likely have a case — and the evaluation is free. Call or text (229) 896-4513, tell us what happened, and an Adel injury attorney will give you an honest answer the same day.

What does it cost to hire you?

Nothing up front. We work on contingency, which means we only get paid if you recover money. The consultation is free, we advance the case costs, and if we don't win, you owe us nothing.

How long will my case take?

Most injury cases in Cook County settle in six to twelve months. Serious cases that go to trial can take longer. We move as fast as your medical recovery allows — settling before you know your full damages costs you money.

The insurance company already offered me money. Should I take it?

Not before a lawyer looks at it. First offers are almost always a fraction of what the case is worth. Send us the offer — reviewing it is free, and you may be leaving serious money on the table.

Will I have to go to court?

Probably not. Most of our South Georgia cases settle out of court. But insurance companies pay more when they know your lawyer will actually try the case — and we do. Either way, you decide whether to settle.
