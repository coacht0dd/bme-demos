Straight Answers

Questions RV Buyers Ask Us

Do you inspect RVs at the dealership or seller's location?

Yes. Vital Inspections Plus travels to wherever your RV is located — the dealership, a private seller's property, a storage lot, or a campground anywhere in Southeast Wisconsin. Call or text (262) 332-0395 and we'll confirm your location works.

What types of RVs do you inspect?

We inspect all motorized and towable RV styles: Class A, B, C, and Super C motorhomes, fifth wheels, toy haulers, and travel trailers — new or used. NRVIA's Standards of Practice cover 100+ inspection points on every rig.

How soon can I get a report after the inspection?

Your detailed inspection report — with photos and plain-English explanations — is delivered within 24 hours of the completed inspection. Lee stays available by phone to walk you through anything in the report.

Is it worth inspecting a new RV from a dealer?

Absolutely. New RVs frequently leave the factory with defects, water intrusion, and improperly installed systems. A pre-delivery inspection is how you make the dealer fix issues before you take possession — not after you've driven it home.

Do you serve areas outside Burlington, WI?

Yes. Our primary service area covers Burlington, Lake Geneva, Kenosha, Racine, Delavan, Elkhorn, Twin Lakes, and surrounding Walworth, Racine, and Kenosha counties. For referred clients outside the area, call (262) 332-0395 to discuss accommodations.
