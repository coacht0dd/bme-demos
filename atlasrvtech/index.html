Questions RVers Ask Us

Do you really come to my campsite or driveway?

Yes — Atlas RV Tech is a fully mobile service. We come to your RV wherever it is: campsite, RV park, storage lot, or your driveway anywhere in Santa Rosa, Sonoma County, and within 50 miles. No towing, no shop, no wait. Call or text us at 1-888-842-2126 with your location.

What types of RVs do you work on?

All five types — Class A, Class B, and Class C motorhomes, fifth wheels, and towable travel trailers. From compact van conversions to full-size diesel pushers, Steven has the multi-certification and hands-on experience to handle any platform.

How fast can you get to me in Sonoma County?

We're based in Santa Rosa, so most Sonoma County service calls are answered the same day. Text us a photo of the problem at 1-888-842-2126 and we'll give you our next available time — often sooner than you'd expect.

Do you give prices up front?

Yes. Every job gets a clear, upfront quote before any work begins — no hidden fees, no surprise invoices. Steven has built his reputation on transparent pricing, which is why 116 reviewers gave him a 4.9-star average.

What is an NRVIA pre-purchase inspection and do I need one?

It's a comprehensive multi-system inspection by a certified NRVIA Level 2 inspector, resulting in a detailed written report with photographs and fluid analysis. If you're buying any RV — new or used — from a dealer or private seller, yes, you need one. It typically reveals issues that save you far more than the inspection costs. Request yours at atlasrvinspections.com or text us at 1-888-842-2126.
